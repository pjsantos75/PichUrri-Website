Our Dinning Spaces

Mariachis Room

Do you like Mariachis?

Experience an evening of fine dining with a view and lively mariachi music. Enjoy a delicious Mexican meal at our Mariachis dining room. Afterwards, sing and dance to mariachi music.

Frida Room

Want to meet Frida?

Experience a memorable dinner evening in our Frida dinning room, enjoying an immersive experience about the biography and life of Frida Kahlo.

Mayas Room

Do you know Maya civilization?

Treat your family or friends to an immersive dinner in a Mayan environment, and get to know the culture and customs of an ancient civilization.

Riviera Room

How about a meal with a sea view?

The best is the Riviera terrace, where you can enjoy the best of local cuisine. Here, the warm nights are lit up and have live music and shows.
